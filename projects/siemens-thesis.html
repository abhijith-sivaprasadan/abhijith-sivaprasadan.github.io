Siemens Energy CFD/CHT and Test Validation Thesis

Thermal-fluid modelling and measurement-chain validation work connected to high-temperature gas-turbine-related rig hardware.

Project snapshot

  • Organisation: Siemens Energy AB, Fluid Dynamic Lab, Finspång
  • Tools: ANSYS Fluent, k-omega SST, NI-DAQ, LabVIEW, Siemens NX, technical documentation
  • Focus: CFD/CHT, pressure-loss analysis, thermal delivery, thermocouple/pressure measurement, validation planning, design iteration

What I did

  • Commissioned and verified NI-DAQ measurement-chain elements for preliminary rig tests, including thermocouple and static/dynamic pressure channels.
  • Developed steady-state compressible CFD/CHT models in ANSYS Fluent using k-omega SST turbulence modelling.
  • Compared baseline and redesign cases under consistent boundary conditions, with mesh/near-wall checks, sensitivity studies and KPI definitions.
  • Documented assumptions and limitations, then defined next-step validation needs for transient/pulsating operation and experimental verification.

Relevance

Why this matters

This work strengthens my modelling profile with practical engineering validation experience: connecting simulations, measurements, uncertainty, test rigs and documentation.
