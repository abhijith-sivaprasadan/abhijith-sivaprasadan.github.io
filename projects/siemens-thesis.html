Siemens Energy CFD/CHT and Test Validation Thesis

Thermal-fluid modelling and test-validation work connected to high-temperature energy equipment and rig-level measurement workflows.

Project snapshot

  • Organisation: Siemens Energy AB, Fluid Dynamic Lab, Finspång
  • Tools: ANSYS Fluent, NI-DAQ, LabVIEW, Siemens NX, technical documentation
  • Focus: CFD/CHT, pressure/temperature measurement, validation planning, design iteration

What I did

  • Commissioned and verified parts of a measurement chain using NI-DAQ, thermocouples/pressure sensors and LabVIEW-based acquisition workflows.
  • Performed steady-state compressible CFD/CHT simulation using ANSYS Fluent and k-ω SST turbulence modelling.
  • Worked with mesh, near-wall treatment, sensitivity checks and KPI-driven design iteration.
  • Prepared documentation and validation planning for later transient/pulsating operation.

Relevance

Why this matters

This work strengthens my modelling profile with practical engineering validation experience: connecting simulations, measurements, uncertainty, test rigs and documentation.
