District Heating Dispatch Optimisation

Multi-asset district-heating dispatch model using linear/mixed-integer optimisation to compare cost, emissions and operational strategies.

Project snapshot

  • Tools: Python, PuLP, pandas, plotting and KPI reporting
  • Focus: CHP, heat pumps, biomass/geothermal assets, storage, outages, electricity-price and CO2 coupling
  • Type: energy-system optimisation project

What I did

  • Built an optimisation workflow for multi-asset heat supply scheduling under demand and capacity constraints.
  • Represented operational constraints such as min/max output, binary on/off logic and outage windows.
  • Compared cost-minimisation and emissions-aware dispatch outcomes.
  • Exported results, plots and KPI summaries for scenario interpretation.

Relevance

Why this matters

This project demonstrates transferable optimisation skills relevant to BESS dispatch, energy-market modelling, scenario analysis and decision-support tools.
