Alleima Energy Efficiency Mapping and KPI Framework

Energy performance mapping, KPI/EnPI design, metering-readiness thinking and improvement-roadmap development for industrial energy efficiency.

Project snapshot

  • Organisation: Alleima AB
  • Focus: energy performance mapping, energy KPIs, normalisation, metering readiness
  • Related standards: ISO 50001 / energy-audit thinking / EU energy-efficiency context

What I did

  • Mapped energy-performance logic and improvement opportunities across utilities and process-energy areas.
  • Developed KPI/EnPI thinking for baselining, drivers, normalisation and data-readiness assessment.
  • Prepared a comparative regulatory analysis around Sweden, EU energy efficiency requirements and ISO 50001-style audit methods.
  • Proposed a tiered audit methodology and quantified savings framework.

Relevance

Why this matters

The work shows how I translate messy industrial energy data and regulatory context into practical KPI structures, measurement plans and management-facing improvement roadmaps.
