Siemens Energy · Test engineering · CFD/CHT

Test Engineer / Master's Thesis Student - Siemens Energy AB

Measurement-chain commissioning and thermo-fluid modelling work for high-temperature gas-turbine-related rig hardware.

Role snapshot

  • Organisation: Siemens Energy AB, Fluid Dynamic Lab, Finspång
  • Period: Apr 2025 - Nov 2025
  • Tools: ANSYS Fluent, k-omega SST, NI-DAQ, LabVIEW, Siemens NX, technical documentation
  • Measurements: thermocouples, static pressure and dynamic pressure channels
  • Focus: measurement chains, pressure loss, thermal delivery, validation planning, design iteration

What I contributed

  • Commissioned and verified NI-DAQ measurement-chain elements, including channel wiring checks and LabVIEW acquisition workflow refinement.
  • Developed steady-state compressible CFD/CHT models in ANSYS Fluent to quantify pressure losses and thermal delivery under consistent boundary conditions.
  • Established a repeatable simulation workflow with mesh/near-wall checks, sensitivity studies, KPI definitions and documented assumptions.
  • Translated simulation outputs into design recommendations and next-step plans for transient/pulsating validation and experimental verification.

Transferable value

Why this experience matters

This role connects modelling with real test constraints: instrumentation, uncertainty, validation planning, documentation and communication between simulation, design iteration and hardware workflows.
