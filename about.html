Working style

Validate assumptions early

Prefer clear boundary conditions, documented limitations and sanity checks before scaling a model or a test setup.

Connect analysis to hardware

Keep instrumentation, test constraints and model interpretation tied together instead of treating simulation as a standalone exercise.

Move between disciplines

Switch between thermal-fluid engineering, energy systems and software implementation without changing the standard of evidence.
