Thesis

MJ232X 30 ECTS

Degree Project in Heat and Power Technology

Independent thesis requiring the student to solve an engineering problem with significant technical content. This thesis (TRITA-ITM-EX 2026:14) was conducted at Siemens Energy Finspång: compressible CFD and conjugate heat transfer analysis of a reducer for the Pulsatorn high-temperature dynamic pressure sensor calibration rig, comparing legacy two-step geometry against a 150 mm quintic C² redesign. Includes mesh independence, Biot number analysis, NI-DAQ commissioning and a formal root cause analysis of heater failure.

Numerical methods & heat transfer

MJ2515 3 ECTS Grade A

Numerical Heat Transfer in Energy Technology

Finite difference and finite volume methods for heat conduction, convection and advection problems in energy applications. Covers derivation of numerical schemes, stability and error analysis, 1D and 2D steady-state and transient solutions, and an introduction to the Navier–Stokes equations and turbulence modelling. Practical work includes programming numerical solvers and interpreting CFD software output.

Thermodynamic cycles, conventional and renewable power plant configurations, environmental impacts and sustainability assessment. Foundational course for the MSc Sustainable Energy Engineering programme.

B.Tech Mechanical Engineering — APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University

Kerala, India · 2017–2021 · Core mechanical engineering foundation spanning thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, heat transfer, manufacturing, machine design and applied mathematics

35 courses completed 4-year programme SAE Collegiate Club · NSS volunteer

Year 1 — Engineering foundation & mathematics

MA101

Calculus

Single and multivariable calculus: limits, differentiation, integration, sequences and series, partial derivatives and multiple integrals applied to engineering problems.

MA102

Differential Equations

Ordinary differential equations (ODEs): first and higher-order equations, series solutions, Laplace transforms and applications to mechanical vibration and heat transfer.

BE100

Engineering Mechanics

Statics and dynamics: equilibrium of rigid bodies, free body diagrams, friction, kinematics and kinetics of particles and rigid bodies, work-energy and impulse-momentum methods.

BE101-02

Introduction to Mechanical Engineering

Survey of mechanical engineering disciplines, engineering problem-solving methodology, professional ethics and an introduction to major application areas including manufacturing, thermal systems and machine design.

BE103

Introduction to Sustainable Engineering

Sustainability principles in engineering practice: resource efficiency, life-cycle thinking, environmental impact and the role of engineers in addressing global sustainability challenges.

BE110

Engineering Graphics

Technical drawing standards, orthographic and isometric projection, sectional views, dimensioning conventions and introduction to CAD tools for engineering communication.

Year 2 — Core mechanical sciences

MA201

Linear Algebra & Complex Analysis

Matrix operations, eigenvalues, vector spaces, linear transformations, complex functions, Cauchy-Riemann equations, contour integration and conformal mapping.

MA202

Probability, Transforms & Numerical Methods

Probability distributions, statistical inference, Fourier and Z-transforms, numerical solutions of equations, interpolation, numerical differentiation and integration, and iterative solvers for ODEs and linear systems.

ME201

Mechanics of Solids

Stress and strain analysis, material behaviour under axial, shear, bending and torsional loads, Mohr's circle, deflection of beams and columns, and elastic stability.

ME202

Advanced Mechanics of Solids

Extension of solid mechanics to thick-walled cylinders, rotating discs, plasticity, contact stresses, fracture mechanics fundamentals and introduction to finite element concepts.

ME203

Mechanics of Fluids

Fluid statics and dynamics: continuity, momentum and energy equations, internal and external flows, boundary layer theory, pipe flow losses and an introduction to turbulence.

ME204

Thermal Engineering

Thermodynamic cycles for power generation and refrigeration: Rankine, Brayton, Otto, Diesel and refrigeration cycles. Analysis of steam generators, condensers, turbines and pumps.

ME205

Thermodynamics

Classical thermodynamics: laws of thermodynamics, properties of pure substances, gas mixtures, combustion stoichiometry, second-law analysis and exergy concepts.

ME206

Fluid Machinery

Turbomachinery principles: centrifugal pumps, axial and mixed-flow fans, hydraulic turbines, velocity triangles, performance characteristics and cavitation.

ME210

Metallurgy & Materials Engineering

Crystal structure, phase diagrams, heat treatment, mechanical properties testing, ferrous and non-ferrous alloys, failure analysis and selection of engineering materials for thermal and structural applications.

ME220

Manufacturing Technology

Fundamentals of casting, forming, welding, machining and surface treatment processes. Emphasis on process selection, tooling, surface finish and dimensional tolerances.

EE311

Electrical Drives & Control for Automation

DC and AC motor characteristics, variable-speed drives, PLC-based control, sensors and actuators in automated manufacturing and process control systems.

Year 3 — Applied engineering

ME301

Mechanics of Machinery

Kinematics and kinetics of mechanisms: linkages, cams, gears and gear trains; dynamic analysis of rotating machinery, balancing, flywheel design and governor mechanisms.

ME302

Heat & Mass Transfer

Conduction, convection (forced and natural), radiation and combined heat transfer. Dimensionless correlations, heat exchanger design (LMTD and NTU methods), and mass transfer by diffusion and convection.

ME303

Machine Tools & Digital Manufacturing

Conventional and CNC machine tools, cutting mechanics, tool geometry and wear, process planning and introduction to digital manufacturing concepts including CAM and Industry 4.0 context.

ME304

Dynamics of Machinery

Vibration analysis of single and multi-degree-of-freedom systems, free and forced vibration, damping, resonance, critical speeds of rotating shafts and vibration isolation and control.

ME306

Advanced Manufacturing Technology

Non-conventional machining processes (EDM, ECM, laser, water-jet), additive manufacturing, rapid prototyping, precision manufacturing and quality engineering.

ME308

Computer Aided Design & Analysis

Geometric modelling, parametric CAD, assembly design and introduction to FEA using commercial tools (SolidWorks, ANSYS). Covers meshing, boundary conditions, linear static analysis and result interpretation.

ME312

Metrology & Instrumentation

Dimensional measurement, gauging, surface texture analysis, calibration principles, transducers, signal conditioning, data acquisition and measurement uncertainty estimation.

Year 4 — Advanced specialisation & electives

ME401

Design of Machine Elements I

Failure theories, fatigue analysis, design of fasteners, welded joints, springs, shafts and couplings under static and dynamic loading with safety factor methodology.

ME402

Design of Machine Elements II

Design of rolling contact bearings, journal bearings, gears (spur, helical, bevel), brakes, clutches and power screws; tribology and lubrication fundamentals.

ME403

Advanced Energy Engineering

Advanced topics in energy conversion: combined cycle plants, cogeneration, waste heat recovery, renewable integration, fuel cells and energy system optimisation with sustainability metrics.

ME404

Industrial Engineering

Work study, production planning and control, facility layout, inventory management, quality control (SPC, six sigma) and operations research techniques for industrial settings.

ME405

Refrigeration & Air Conditioning

Vapour compression and absorption refrigeration cycles, psychrometrics, cooling load estimation, air conditioning system components and design, cold storage and heat pump applications.

ME407

Mechatronics

Integration of mechanical, electronic and control subsystems: sensors, actuators, microcontrollers, interfacing, feedback control design and real-time embedded systems for automation.

ME409

Compressible Fluid Flow

Thermodynamics of compressible flow: isentropic relations, normal and oblique shocks, Prandtl–Meyer expansion, Fanno and Rayleigh flows, converging-diverging nozzles and supersonic diffusers. Foundation for the gas-turbine CFD thesis work.

ME463

Automobile Engineering

Vehicle dynamics, powertrain systems, transmission, braking, steering geometry, suspension design and emission control technologies for internal combustion engine vehicles.

ME376

Maintenance Engineering

Maintenance strategies (preventive, predictive, condition-based), reliability analysis, failure mode and effect analysis (FMEA), total productive maintenance and life-cycle cost frameworks.

ME476

Material Handling & Facilities Planning

Plant layout planning, material handling systems (conveyors, AGVs, cranes), warehouse design, ergonomic principles and supply chain interface for manufacturing facilities.

CE482

Environmental Impact Assessment

EIA methodology, regulatory frameworks, scoping, baseline studies, impact prediction, mitigation measures and public participation processes for engineering projects.
